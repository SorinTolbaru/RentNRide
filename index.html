Finding your ride with us: FAQs

How does RentNRide car hire comparison tool work?

We use a technology that searches the web for car hire deals from most major providers. So you can see every price in one place, instead of having to search and compare across a dozen different car hire websites. Currently, you'll be able to find the lowest price for car hire in 18,000 pick-up locations globally right here in our search.

Are there any extra fees added to the price advertised?

We account for any extra fees including insurance, local taxes and airport surcharges in the price shown when you search. Many of our insurance partners offer an excess refund option, which may prove cheaper than the rental companies excess waiver offered at the rental counter. Additional drivers or child seats can be purchased when you pick the car up.

Can I return my hired car to a different location?

Yes, some car hire providers like Hertz and Europcar allow you to pick up a car in one location and drop it off in another. Simply select Return car to different location when you search with us. Note that you could be charged a one-way hire fee.

Do I need a cred card in order to rent a car?

Although it might be smoother and faster to hire a car with a credit card, these days most major providers like Sixt, Avis, Budget, Hertz will allow you to hire car with a debit card if you have a certain amount of cash on it – for example £1000.

What kind of vehicle can I hire?

We scan the internet for all kinds of vehicle rental deals, so you'll find everything from cheap and cheerful hatchbacks, to exotic sports cars on Skyscanner. You can even find deals on motor homes or passenger vans, too.

Can I hire a car for just one day?

Yes - most car rental companies offer a flat rate for hire per day. You'll usually have to drop it back within 24 hours of picking it up to avoid any extra charges.

Can I hire a car for a month?

Of course! Simply set your dates to your desired period of time and hit search to find the best deals. And here's an insider tip – some car rental companies try to save costs and avoid short bookings by offering discounts or free days between two longer bookings.

What is the maximum amount of passengers a vehicle can carry?

The largest hire vehicle we've found is a passenger van for 9 people.
